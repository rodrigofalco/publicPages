## Attraction Tickets, Add-ons & Included Values

### Ticket Pick-up Locations

#### Universal Vacation Planning Center
Located in the lobby of any Universal Orlando Resort hotel and select Universal Partner hotels. A team member will assist you in printing tickets and answering questions. Have your reservation and Feature Confirmation number ready.

#### Front Gate Ticket Window
Pick up your park ticket(s) at any front gate ticket window:
- Universal Studios Florida
- Universal Islands of Adventure
- Universal Volcano Bay
- Universal Epic Universe

Available during regular theme park operating hours. Present your Feature Confirmation number(s) and valid photo ID.

### 3-Day Park-to-Park Ticket (Adult)

**Valid Date:** Tuesday, April 14, 2026
**Valid For:** 2 Adults
**Feature Confirmation:** UOM0T779-2UPRV

The 3-Day Park-to-Park Ticket entitles one guest admission to:
- Universal Studios Florida
- Universal Islands of Adventure
- Universal Epic Universe

Ticket is valid for any 3 calendar days over a 6 consecutive calendar day period starting on the selected date.

**Terms:**
- Non-refundable and non-transferable
- Must be used by the same person on all days
- Standard entry includes biometric scan
- Valid only during normal operating hours
- Unused days are forfeited
- Parks/attractions may close or change without notice

*Note: Excludes separately ticketed events, CityWalk venues, parking discounts, and food/merchandise discounts. Valid Photo ID required.*

**Valid For:** 2 Adults

**Requirements:**

- Valid at select attractions at each park
